Moodle Quiz

In your Moodle module you can build a series of questions and add them to your question bank for that module, you can then add these questions to a quiz.
Lab-01

In this lab we look at the different settings in the quiz activity.
Lab-02

In this lab we look at creating questions in the question bank, there are lots of different types of questions and some different ways of importing questions, we will explore the basics here.
Lab-03

This labs looks at how we combine the questions from your question bank and combine them with the quiz you just created.
